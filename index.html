SEINFELD

It’s about nothing… and yet, you can’t stop watching. Seinfeld takes you on a hilarious journey through the trivial, the awkward, and the unforgettable.

Main Characters

Jerry Seinfeld

Played by Jerry Seinfeld

A neurotic stand-up comedian whose life is filled with bizarre encounters and peculiar observations.

George Costanza

Played by Jason Alexander

Jerry's best friend since high school, a short, stocky, slow-witted, bald man who struggles with every aspect of life.

Elaine Benes

Played by Julia Louis-Dreyfus

Jerry's ex-girlfriend turned friend, a smart and assertive woman who often finds herself in awkward situations.

Cosmo Kramer

Played by Michael Richards

Jerry's eccentric neighbor, known for his wild hair, unusual schemes, and physical comedy.
